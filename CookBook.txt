Data Dictionary : Samsung Wearable Study Summary Tidy Dataset

Subject : Indicates the number of the subject participated in the study
	  Range from 1 to 30, as the study containg 30 subjects

Activity : Indicates the activity type, which can be LAYING,SITTING,STANDING,WALKING,WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS,WALKING_UPSTAIRS

The average of mean and standard deviation of various measured observed during the study are indicated per each activity and each subject.
There are 33 measures observed during the study and each of these have an average of mean and standard deviation per each activity and each subject.

The measure names are descriptive and also indicate if it is Mean or Standard Deviation
